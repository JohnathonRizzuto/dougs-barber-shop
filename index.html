Meet Doug
The Man Behind the Chair

Same Doug.
Same Chair.
Half a Century.

Doug has been cutting hair in Somonauk for over fifty years — long enough that he's cut three generations of the same family, and you'll still walk in to find him ready with a joke and a story before he picks up the clippers.

This is the only barbershop inside Somonauk village limits, and it's been that way for as long as most folks can remember. No frills, no fads. Just an honest haircut from a guy who's seen everything come and go and still does the work right.

"Folks don't come here for a fancy salon. They come for a real haircut and a few good laughs."
